FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

When will I get my refund?

Once your return arrives at one of our warehouses and the quality has been approved, we’ll initiate your refund.

If you chose an e-gift card as the refund method, you’ll receive this via email immediately after your return was approved.

Did you choose to get a refund issued to your original payment method? Then it can take up to 14 business days until you see the amount on your account, depending on your bank.

How do I return my product(s)?

Most items can be returned for free within 30 days after receiving your order.

Everything you return should be in good condition. Make sure you don’t cut off tags if there are any. Pack all items in their original packaging.

Start a return on our website or on the app and follow the instructions. You’ll receive all the details you need before dropping off your parcel at the drop-off point.

Did you buy something in one of our stores? Please return to the store to return or exchange it in person.

Can I exchange my product(s)?

Ordered the wrong size or color? You can exchange most items for free within 30 days after receiving your order.

Everything you exchange should be in its original state and packaging. Make sure you don’t cut off tags if there are any.

Start an exchange on our website or the app and follow the instructions. You’ll receive all the details you need before dropping off your package at the drop-off point.

Make sure to drop off your parcel within 10 days after letting us know you want to exchange the item. Exchange with the carrier that’s mentioned on your exchange label to avoid costs.
